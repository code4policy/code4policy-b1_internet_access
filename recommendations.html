As an international donor, how can you work with the following actors to address the main barrier of affordability?

Policymakers and regulators

Development community

To lower the cost of internet enabled mobile phones

  • Partner to offer entry-level smartphones, or smart feature phones, to customers at a reduced cost
  • Provide microloans or instalment repayment plans with third parties

To help trigger mobile internet adoption

  • Offer subsidies for low-cost smartphones
  • Partner to adapt products and services to make them more affordable without compromising on quality

To reduce institutional barriers of affordability

  • Partner to review the impact of Universal Service Funds on the affordability of mobile and mobile internet services for women
  • Partner to support financial institutions to provide risk capital for handset loans for women at lower interest rates

To create ecosystem promoting handset affordability

  • Partner to provide handset financing schemes provided through local NGO networks and grassroots networks led by women
